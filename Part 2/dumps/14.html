      The Perfect Matte (Face) Concealer, Worth The Hype3 months ago
      The Good: I've been using this concealer since it first dropped and got it's initial hype and this is one of the only products in recent history that has ...More
      The Good: I've been using this concealer since it first dropped and got it's initial hype and this is one of the only products in recent history that has truly and wholeheartedly deserved it's fan rage. I will continue buying this and always compare concealers to this holy grail product, most of them do not measure up. The matte version of this concealer is a nice creamy-liquid formula that is quite hydrating when it first meets the skin, however once blended out and dried it showcases it's mattifying effects. The regular (matte) version of shape tape is truly a MATTE concealer and is perfect for oily, acne-prone skin. I especially enjoy this concealer in the summer when my combo skin leans oily and find that it's lasting powers always outweigh whatever foundation I'm wearing (especially on a particularly hot day), therefore in the summer I've used this as a foundation as well. When I really need something to stay put on my face, this is truly my go-to most reached for all around concealer. The shade rage is stunning (I own it in 3 shades) and no matter how pale or tan my skin is throughout the yearly changes I always can find my exact match. This is also a truly high coverage concealer, this covers any redness or dark spots flawlessly. I absolutely love this stuff and will continue to re-buy.The Bad: I tend to not use this in my under eye area unless I really need the intense pigment payoff from this because it's quite mattifying which can accentuate any kind of fine lines or wrinkles.The Ugly/Honest: The price of this has been continuously rising and it was already a pricey product. I run through this in the summer time and it comes with a hefty price. The applicator is super large for a concealer which makes it hard to control the amount of product used in one area and is overall unnecessarily large. I wish this concealer was more under-eye friendly, considering that's where most people use concealer on. Per my mom, this is not compatible with mature skin.
      QueenMarceline · Reviewed on Sephora
