      I have mixed feeling about this palette. Some shades are a miss for me (conch especially), and 2 of the shades are wayyy too similar (Marina Seaside - even though I do like how both of them look). At the same time, I really love Marina, Seaside, Siren, Breezy, and Riptide. I’ve never felt shadows like these in a palette, and at first it really threw me off. They’re not really powder shadows, so there is a learning curve. You can still apply them with a brush, you just have to really dig that brush into the pan in order to pick up enough Pigment. The benefit is that the shades melt right into your skin, they blend beautifully, and they give off a unique finish. I’d almost recommend applying these shadows with those old-school sponge eyeshadow applicators. I do recommend this palette.
      alessi.m · Reviewed on influenster.com
