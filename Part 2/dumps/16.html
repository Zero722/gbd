      I absolutely love the way this foundation makes my skin look hydrated and pretty much flawless however I can't get it to stay on my face and I've no idea ...More
      I absolutely love the way this foundation makes my skin look hydrated and pretty much flawless however I can't get it to stay on my face and I've no idea what I'm doing wrong. I have normal to slightly combination skin with a bit of oil on my chin. I've used this foundation on its own, with primer, setting powder, setting spray although nothing seems to help keep it in place. The foundation itself makes my skin look radiance and healthy but wipes off at the slightest touch or starts to move on my face especially separating around my chin and cheeks. I have used face tape concealer since it's release and have never any this issue with that product. I have tried a change in moisturiser and SPF, which I use everyday, however have not seen an improvement. If I wear a mask the entire bottom half of my face is makeup-less very quickly. This happens when I use a foundation brush or sponge. The same happens when I use the Babassu Foundcealer, which I also love the finish of, but cannot wear anymore as it slides off my face. The other two makeup brand foundations I use have similar finishes although I've not experienced this slipping and sliding before - I really want to avoid using a large amount of finishing powder as it ages my skin and can make me look dehydrated. I'm not sure if I'm doing something wrong or of this and the babassu foundation just aren't for me. Any advice from Tarte with usage tips or alternates that might stay on my face would be really helpful. It's a real shame because the finish of this foundation on my skin is perfect!
      Sarah  Reviewed on tartecosmetics.com
